TOWER OF HANOI

Welcome to Tower of Hanoi

To win, you must move each disc, one at a time, from the starting block to the target block. You may only place a disc on top another that is LARGER than itself, or on an empty block. The game is over if you can re-stack all the discs in the original starting order, or large to small, bottom to top. GOOD LUCK!!!
